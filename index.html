About Me

I am a graduate of the Technical Communication program at Simon Fraser University and the Full-Stack Web Development program at York University. I have also taken art and technology courses at the Ontario College of Art Design University, Emily Carr University of Art + Design, and the University of Toronto.

I am great at turning complex subject matter into user-friendly content using clear, plain language. I have written about hardware and software products for various audiences, including troubleshooting workflows for support teams and help procedures for external users.

I can build strong relationships with SMEs and know how to listen and what questions to ask to develop accurate, helpful content.

I am also passionate about organizing information and have experience managing a technical support knowledge base.
